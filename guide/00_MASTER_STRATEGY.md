## Executive Summary

The **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023** comes into force on **13 May 2024** (effective date).

This Master Strategy document provides a complete **6-phase implementation roadmap** for achieving DPDP compliance across your organization. It covers:

- **50 compliance templates** (T-01 to T-50) organized into 6 phases
- **92 compliance checkpoints** tracked across 17 domains
- **3 compliance tracks** (Quick Start, Full Program, FinTech-Specific)
- **Roles, timelines, and success criteria** per phase
- **DPDP penalty exposure framework** (₹50-100 Crore per violation)

**Target Outcome:** 100% DPDP compliance by 13 May 2027 (3-year grace period).

---

## Why This Matters: Penalty Exposure

| Violation | Section | Penalty Range | Individuals Affected |
|-----------|---------|---|---|
| **Unauthorized consent collection** | S.6 | ₹50-100 Cr | All customers with non-consensual data processing |
| **Data breach (no notification)** | S.8(6) | ₹50-100 Cr | Breach victims; RBI/DPBI escalation |
| **DSAR non-compliance (>30d)** | S.11-12 | ₹50-100 Cr | Data principals unable to access their data |
| **Security control failure** | S.8 | ₹50-100 Cr | Entire customer base at risk |
| **Unauthorized processor data sharing** | S.8(2) | ₹50-100 Cr | Customers; third parties |
| **Children's data mishandling** | S.9 | ₹50-100 Cr | Minor users; parental claims |
| **Breach notification delay (>72h)** | S.8(6) | Up to ₹250 Cr (per breach event) | All affected individuals |

**Non-compliance by 13 May 2027:** No deadline enforcement expected, but regulatory risk accumulates. First compliance audits by DPBI and SDF independent auditors begin mid-2027.

## The 6-Phase Implementation Roadmap

### **Phase 0: Scoping (Weeks -2 to 0) — January 2026**
- **Applicability Test:** Are you a Data Fiduciary (DF) or Significant Data Fiduciary (SDF)?
- **Entity Classification:** Which industry entities (fintech, health, insurance, employer, etc.)?
- **Regulatory Overlay:** RBI, SEBI, PMLA, IRDAI, healthcare regulations?
- **Deliverables:** Applicability matrix, entity type assignment
- **Templates:** T-01, T-02
- **Effort:** 1-2 weeks | **Owner:** Legal, CTO

### **Phase 1: Assessment (Weeks 1-8) — January-March 2026**
- **Data Inventory:** Audit all systems holding personal data
- **ROPA:** Record of Processing Activities per Section 5
- **Data Flows:** Map how data moves between systems
- **Consent & Notice Review:** Audit existing consent, privacy notices, terms
- **Deliverables:** Data inventory, ROPA document, data flow diagrams
- **Templates:** T-03 to T-09
- **Effort:** 6-8 weeks | **Owner:** DPO, Data Governance, Product

### **Phase 2: Gap Analysis (Weeks 3-8, overlapping) — February-May 2026**
- **17-Domain Scoring:** Assess compliance per domain (Consent, Security, DSAR, etc.)
- **Heat Map:** Visualize gaps and risk areas
- **Penalty Exposure:** Calculate risk by violation type
- **Remediation Roadmap:** Prioritize by penalty exposure
- **Deliverables:** Gap register, compliance heat map, remediation priorities
- **Templates:** T-25, T-28
- **Effort:** 4-6 weeks | **Owner:** DPO, Compliance, Legal

### **Phase 3: Remediation (Weeks 6-16) — March-June 2026**
- **Consent Management:** Deploy FSIUU-compliant consent forms (T-08)
- **Data Subject Rights:** Build DSAR, correction, erasure workflows (T-10 to T-14)
- **Vendor Compliance:** DPA contracts, processor risk assessments (T-15, T-16)
- **Security Controls:** Encryption, access control, audit logging (T-24, T-33, T-35)
- **Breach Response:** Incident response plan, SIEM/DAM setup (T-20, T-21, T-21A)
- **Governance:** Board resolutions, DPO charter (T-39, T-40)
- **Deliverables:** Deployed consent system, DSAR workflows, signed DPAs, security audit report
- **Templates:** T-07 to T-29
- **Effort:** 10-12 weeks | **Owner:** Product, IT, Security, Legal, Procurement

### **Phase 4: Monitoring & Live Dashboards (Weeks 9-20) — May-September 2026**
- **Consent Registry:** T-31 dashboard with health metrics
- **Data Discovery:** T-32 automated PII scanning
- **Audit Logging:** T-35 immutable logs with PII redaction
- **Compliance Dashboard:** T-38 0-100% compliance score tracking
- **KPI Monitoring:** Withdrawal rates, DSAR SLA, breach indicators
- **Deliverables:** Live dashboards, automated data discovery, audit log pipeline
- **Templates:** T-31 to T-38
- **Effort:** 8-10 weeks | **Owner:** IT, Analytics, Compliance

### **Phase 5: Governance & Certification (Weeks 16-24) — June-October 2026**
- **Privacy Committee:** T-39 charter, quarterly reviews
- **Board Certification:** T-40 board resolution, T-41 compliance opinion letter
- **Annual Audit (SDF):** Independent auditor audit per Section 10
- **Ongoing Obligations:** T-45 annual task calendar
- **DPBI Readiness:** Documentation for Data Protection Board audits
- **Deliverables:** Board-signed compliance certificate, independent audit report, DPO certification
- **Templates:** T-39 to T-48
- **Effort:** 6-8 weeks | **Owner:** Board, Legal, DPO

## Compliance Tracks

### **Track A: Quick Start (DF Only, <6 months)**
- Applicability test (T-01)
- Privacy notice update (T-07)
- Consent form FSIUU fix (T-08)
- DSAR SOP (T-11, T-12)
- Breach notification SOP (T-21, T-22)
- **Effort:** 4-6 weeks | **Budget:** ₹5-10L | **Risk:** High (limited audit trail)

### **Track B: Full Program (DF + Minimal SDF, 9-12 months)**
- Complete Phase 1-5 roadmap (Phases 0-5)
- 50-template library deployment
- Privacy committee + annual audit
- **Effort:** 12-16 weeks | **Budget:** ₹20-40L | **Risk:** Low (comprehensive)

### **Track C: FinTech-Specific (NBFC/Fintech SDF, 12-18 months)**
- Full Program + additional financial compliance
- RBI KYC/AML overlay (10-year retention)
- AA (Account Aggregator) Framework integration
- Breach notification (DPBI + CERT-In dual-timer)
- **Effort:** 16-24 weeks | **Budget:** ₹40-80L | **Risk:** Minimal (regulatory expert)

## Critical Success Factors

1. **DPO Appointment** (if SDF): Required before Phase 1 assessment
2. **Board Sponsorship:** Compliance requires cross-functional executive buy-in
3. **Timeline Discipline:** 3-year grace period ends 13 May 2027; no extensions expected
4. **Vendor Onboarding:** DPA signing timeline can add 4-6 weeks (push early)
5. **Technology Readiness:** Consent management, SIEM, audit logging require 8-12 weeks setup
6. **Staff Training:** Annual DPDP training mandatory for all roles handling personal data
