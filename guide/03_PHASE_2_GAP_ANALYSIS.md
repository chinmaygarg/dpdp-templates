## Gap Severity Classification

| Severity | Criteria | Penalty Exposure | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Missing controls for high-risk processing (consent, security, breach notification) | ₹50-100 Cr | Remediate within 30 days |
| **High** | Incomplete implementation of mandatory requirements | ₹25-50 Cr | Remediate within 60 days |
| **Medium** | Minor gaps or missing documentation | ₹10-25 Cr | Remediate within 90 days |
| **Low** | Non-material gaps or process improvements | <₹10 Cr | Plan remediation; no deadline |
