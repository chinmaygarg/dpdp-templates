# Phase 3: Remediation — Consent, Rights, Vendor, Security, Breach

**Duration:** Weeks 6-16 (March-June 2026) | **Effort:** 10-12 weeks | **Owner:** Product, IT, Security, Legal, Procurement | **Templates:** T-07 to T-29

---

## Overview

Phase 3 closes all identified gaps by implementing DPDP-required controls across 5 streams.

---

## Stream 1A: Consent & Privacy Notice (T-07, T-08)

- **Privacy Notice (T-07):** Update version, deploy on website, notify customers
- **Consent Form (T-08):** Implement FSIUU-compliant form with per-purpose checkboxes
- **Success Criteria:** ≥98% of new consents meet FSIUU (5/5 validity score)

## Stream 1B: Data Subject Rights (T-10 to T-14)

| Right | Template | Action | SLA |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Access** | T-10 Form, T-11 Letter | DSAR portal + 30-day response | 30 days |
| **Correction** | T-12 SOP | In-app correction + notification | 15 days |
| **Erasure** | T-12 SOP + T-29 Log | Automated deletion + confirmation | 30 days |
| **Grievance** | T-13 SOP | Escalation to DPBI if unresolved | 30 days + 60 day DPBI review |
| **Nomination** | T-14 Form | Nominee registration (will exercise rights if deceased) | 15 days |

**Success Criteria:** All DSAR responses within 30 days; 99%+ SLA compliance.

## Stream 2: Vendor & Processor Management (T-15, T-16)

| Activity | Template | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **DPA Negotiation** | T-15 | Sign DPA with all processors; include Section 12(4) propagation + audit rights |
| **Risk Assessment** | T-16 | Complete VRA for all vendors >medium risk |
| **Sub-Processor Clause** | T-15 | Ensure no sub-processor engagement without 30-day notice |
| **Audit Rights** | T-15 | Right to audit processor security controls |

**Success Criteria:** 100% DPA coverage; zero unsigned DPAs; zero high-risk vendors (>70 score).

## Stream 3: Governance & Policies (T-18, T-39, T-40)

- **T-18:** Master Data Protection Policy (staff accountability)
- **T-39:** Privacy Committee Charter (board governance)
- **T-40:** Board Resolution (formal DPDP compliance mandate)

**Success Criteria:** Board-signed resolution; Privacy Committee holding quarterly meetings.

## Stream 4A: Employee Training (T-19)

- Mandatory DPDP training for all staff (2-hour course)
- Role-specific modules (IT, HR, Customer Service, Executive)
- Annual refresher training
- Completion rate target: ≥95%

**Template:** T-19 Employee Training Program

## Stream 4B: Security & Breach Response (T-20 to T-24)

| Control | Template | Action | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Incident Response** | T-20 | Document 6-step breach playbook; conduct tabletop exercise | CISO |
| **DPBI Notification** | T-21 | Procedure for 72-hour DPBI notification | DPO |
| **CERT-In Notification** | T-21A | Procedure for 6-hour CERT-In cybersecurity notification | CISO |
| **Customer Notification** | T-22 | Breach notification letter template | Legal |
| **Post-Breach Review** | T-23 | Root cause analysis; control improvement plan | CISO |
| **Security Hardening** | T-24 | AES-256 encryption, SIEM, MFA, access controls | CISO |

**Success Criteria:** All breach events notified to DPBI and affected principals within SLA.

## Stream 4C: Data Lifecycle (T-02A, T-29, T-30)

- **T-02A:** Data Retention Schedule (how long data is kept per category)
- **T-29:** Deletion Log (audit trail of erasures)
- **T-30:** Breach Register (incident tracking)

**Success Criteria:** Automated deletion working; 100% of statutory holds documented.
