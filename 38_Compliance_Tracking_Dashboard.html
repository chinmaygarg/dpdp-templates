COMPLIANCE SCORE METHODOLOGY (0-100%)

Overall Compliance Score = (Checkpoints Completed / 92 Total Checkpoints) × 100

The 92 checkpoints represent all mandatory DPDP Act 2023 requirements. Checkpoints are weighted by domain importance and penalty exposure. This dashboard tracks progress toward 100% compliance (target deadline: 13 May 2027).
