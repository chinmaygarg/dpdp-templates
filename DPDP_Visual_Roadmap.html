Milestones

Key Milestones & Deliverables

Week 0
Engagement Letter Signed
Formal start of engagement. Scoping document and timeline finalized.
Week 4
Assessment Report Delivered
Complete data inventory, flow diagrams, consent audit, security baseline.
Week 8
Gap Analysis Report + Roadmap Approved
17-Domain scorecard, risk matrix, remediation roadmap reviewed by stakeholders.
Week 24
All Remediation Activities Complete
Governance, notices, consent, rights, vendors, breach, and security hardening done.
Week 32
Internal Audit Passed, No Critical Findings
Independent audit completed. All critical and high issues remediated.
Week 40
Compliance Certificate Issued
Final compliance opinion issued. Evidence pack and obligations calendar handed off.
