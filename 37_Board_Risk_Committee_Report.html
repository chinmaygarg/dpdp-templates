1. Purpose & Audience (SDF Governance)

Mandate: DPDP Act 2023 Section 10 (SDF only) requires quarterly governance reporting to Board Privacy Committee on compliance health, risks, and breaches.

Audience: Board Audit Committee, Independent Director (Privacy Expert), CEO, CFO, DPO (non-voting)

Frequency: Quarterly (Q1 end-March, Q2 end-June, Q3 end-September, Q4 end-December) + Emergency sessions if CRITICAL breach/gap

5. Emergency Session Triggers (Out-of-Cycle Escalation)

If ANY of these occur, Board Privacy Committee convenes within 48 hours:
• Data Breach with >10,000 principals affected → Board notification, DPBI escalation plan
• Compliance Score drops >10% in one quarter → Root cause analysis, remediation plan
• DSAR/Deletion SLA missed for >5 individuals → Escalation risk assessment, legal review
• DPA unsigned with major vendor handling sensitive data → Vendor replacement timeline
• Regulatory fine/penalty imposed → Legal defense strategy, control improvement
• Audit finding CRITICAL → Immediate remediation plan + resource allocation

Note: Emergency session is separate from scheduled quarterly report. Minutes documented. DPO must brief CEO within 24 hours.
