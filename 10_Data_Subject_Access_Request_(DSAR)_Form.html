About This Form

Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to submit a Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. This form is free to submit and you will receive an acknowledgement within 48 hours and a full response within 30 days.

STEP 2: Select Your Data Rights (Tick All That Apply)

You can request one or multiple rights in a single DSAR. Each request will be processed within 30 days.

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, including: contact information, financial details, transaction history, consent records, communications, preferences, and any other data we process about you.
What you'll receive: CSV file, JSON export, and PDF summary (choose format below)
If any of your personal data is inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading, request that we correct it immediately.
What to do: Provide details of the inaccurate data and the correction needed below (under "Additional Details").
Timeline: Corrected within 30 days. We will notify all processors/vendors we shared data with (within 5 days of your correction).
Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions (if required by law, ongoing litigation, or regulatory holds).
Important: Some data may be retained for legal/statutory reasons (e.g., loan records for 8 years per RBI).
Timeline: Deleted within 30 days. We will notify all vendors/processors to delete data within 5 business days of receipt.
Exception: Data retained for legal holds will be documented separately.
Request your personal data in a machine-readable format (CSV, JSON, Excel) so you can transfer it to another organization or service provider.
What you'll receive: Structured data export in open formats (JSON, CSV, XML). Choose format below.
Timeline: Provided within 30 days.
Designate a nominee (family member, legal representative) who can exercise your data rights after your death.
What to do: Provide nominee details in "Additional Details" section (name, relationship, contact, ID proof scans).
Timeline: Processed within 30 days. You will receive nomination confirmation.
If you believe we have violated your data rights under DPDP, you can file a complaint for investigation.
Complaint details: Describe the violation, when it occurred, and what you'd like us to do.
Timeline: Acknowledged within 48 hours. Investigation completed within 30 days. Unresolved grievances escalated to DPBI (Data Protection Board of India).
