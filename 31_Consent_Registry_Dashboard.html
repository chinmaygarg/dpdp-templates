CONSENT REGISTRY HEALTH SCORING

The Consent Registry tracks all consent records and calculates health metrics to identify compliance risks. A "healthy" consent registry demonstrates that consent is being collected, managed, and withdrawn properly per DPDP Act Section 6 (FSIUU framework).

Consent Registry Health Score Methodology

Overall Health Score = 100 - (Per-Purpose Validity Issues + Withdrawal Compliance Issues + Invalid Consent Issues)

Health Metric Calculation / Formula Target / Benchmark Alert Threshold Mitigation Action
1. Per-Purpose Consent Coverage (%) (Count of consents with explicit per-purpose checkboxes / Total consents) × 100
Example: 950 consents have PC-001 (Acct Mgmt) separated from PC-003 (Marketing) out of 1,000 total = 95% 		Target: ≥98% Alert if <95% Audit Consent Form (T-08) implementation; re-deploy if form not showing per-purpose checkboxes; re-collect consents if <5% separation
2. Withdrawal Compliance Rate (%) (Count of withdrawals processed within 7 days / Total withdrawal requests) × 100
Example: 48 out of 50 withdrawals processed within 7 days = 96% 		Target: ≥99% Alert if <95% Review withdrawal SOP (T-08 DSAR SOP); ensure CPaaS vendor suppression list sync within 24hr; escalate delayed withdrawals to DPO
3. Consent Withdrawal Rate (Risk) (Count of withdrawn consents per purpose / Total active consents for that purpose) × 100
Example: 150 withdrawals for Marketing (PC-003) out of 1,000 active = 15% withdrawal rate 		Target: <10% ⚠️ CRITICAL ALERT if >15% HIGH RISK: >15% withdrawal suggests either (a) non-consensual initial consent (FSIUU violated), or (b) data principals don't trust your use. Review: Consent form messaging, CPaaS suppression accuracy, withdrawal UX friction, data usage transparency. Escalate to Board Privacy Committee.
4. Invalid Consent Rate (%) (Count of consents failing FSIUU validity check / Total consents) × 100

FSIUU Validity Check (all 5 must = 1 point each, ÷ 5 for score):
• Free? (not bundled with service)
• Specific? (per-purpose, not blanket)
• Informed? (privacy notice provided)
• Unconditional? (not tied to service access)
• Unambiguous? (clear yes/no, not pre-ticked)

Example: 50 consents out of 1,000 score <4/5 = 5% invalid 		Target: <2% 🔴 CRITICAL ALERT if >5% COMPLIANCE FAILURE: >5% invalid consents = Section 6 violation risk. Immediate actions: (1) Audit all flagged consents, (2) Fix consent form FSIUU issues (T-08), (3) Re-collect consents from affected principals, (4) Notify DPO/Legal; document incident in breach register.
5. Section 6 Validity Score (Average) (Sum of all FSIUU scores / Total consent records) / 5 × 100

Example: 1,000 consents, 950 score 5/5 (FSIUU valid), 40 score 4/5, 10 score 3/5
= (950×5 + 40×4 + 10×3) / 1,000 / 5 × 100 = 98.6% 		Target: ≥97% Alert if <95% Review low-scoring consents; identify pattern (e.g., "Informed" failures = privacy notice not shown); fix consent form and retrain staff
6. Consent Registry Completeness (%) (Consents recorded in Consent Registry / Total consents expected per business records) × 100

Example: 10,000 consents from onboarding system, 9,850 recorded in Consent Registry = 98.5% completeness 		Target: 100% Alert if <99% Gap = missing audit trail. Investigate: Data sync failures, consent collection not logging to registry, system outage. Remediate by backfilling missing records with data source verification.

Health Score Interpretation & Response Matrix

Health Score Status Interpretation Required Actions
95-100 HEALTHY Consent processes are working well. FSIUU compliance strong. No immediate red flags. • Continue current practices
• Monthly audit of edge cases
• Board reporting: Green status
90-94 CAUTION Minor issues identified (e.g., per-purpose coverage 96%, validity score 94%). Risks manageable but trend watch needed. • Root cause analysis (which metric is low?)
• Action plan with 30-day deadline
• Bi-weekly DPO reviews
• Board reporting: Amber status with improvement plan
<90 CRITICAL Major compliance gaps (e.g., >5% invalid consents, >15% withdrawal rate, >5% incomplete records). DPDP Section 6 breach risk imminent. • IMMEDIATE escalation to DPO + Board Privacy Committee
• Emergency audit of all flagged consents
• Halt new consent collection if form is broken
• Re-collect consents from affected principals
• Incident documentation for potential DPBI reporting
• Board reporting: Red status; remediation plan
Consent Registry Health Monitoring Schedule:
• Weekly: Dashboard updated every Monday; KPIs calculated from Sunday midnight snapshot
• Monthly: Detailed health report to Board Privacy Committee
• Quarterly: Full FSIUU audit (sample 100 consents; verify all 5 attributes)
• Annual: Board certification of consent registry health; SDF audit sign-off
