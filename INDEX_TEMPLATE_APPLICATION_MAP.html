Research Documentation

Phase 2: Gap Analysis

4 Weeks (Wks 5-8)
17 Compliance Domains
17-Domain Scorecard
T-25
Score each DPDP compliance domain (Consent, Security, DSAR, Retention, Breach, Cross-Border, etc.) against current state. Produces a heat-map of red/amber/green status.
Domain Compliance Scorecard
Gap Analysis Report
T-28
Formal document listing all identified gaps by domain with severity (Critical / High / Medium / Low), penalty exposure, and recommended remediation action.
Prioritised Gap List + Penalty Matrix
Remediation Roadmap
Plan
Phase-by-phase remediation plan with task ownership, effort estimates (hours), dependencies, and target completion week. Maps to Phases 3-5 of this engagement.
Phased Remediation Plan
Consent Audit Worksheet
T-42
Audit existing consent records against FSIUU requirements (Free, Specific, Informed, Unambiguous, Unconditional). Identifies legacy consent that must be re-collected.
FSIUU Compliance Assessment
Effort & Timeline Estimate
Plan
Total effort estimate in person-hours across all phases, cost projection, and milestone schedule aligned to the May 2027 compliance deadline.
Signed Engagement Timeline
Data Discovery Report
T-32
Automated and manual scan to identify all systems and databases holding personal data — including shadow IT, third-party integrations, and unstructured data stores.
Personal Data Inventory

Phase 2 Complete

All 17 domains scored. Gap list accepted by client. Remediation plan signed off. Phase 3 kickoff approved.

Phase 3: Remediation

16 Weeks (Wks 9-24)
7 Sub-Phases (Parallel)

3.1 — Governance Setup (Weeks 9-12)

Data Protection Policy
T-18
Master organisational data governance policy covering all personal data processing, security obligations, breach response, and staff responsibilities under the DPDP Act.
Board-Approved Policy Document
Privacy Committee Charter
T-39
Establishes the Privacy Committee: membership (DPO, Legal, CISO, Compliance), meeting cadence, decision authority, escalation paths, and reporting to the Board.
Signed Charter + Committee Minutes
DPO Appointment Letter
T-27
Formal appointment of Data Protection Officer (mandatory for Significant Data Fiduciaries). Defines role, India-based requirement, board reporting obligations, and independence safeguards.
Signed Appointment Letter (SDF Only)
Employee Training Programme
T-19
Mandatory staff training covering DPDP obligations, consent handling, breach reporting procedures, and data minimisation. Includes completion tracking and refresh schedule.
Training Completion Register
Employee Data Protection Policy
T-03A
Governing policy for how employee personal data is collected, used, shared, and deleted. Covers 10 data categories (payroll, KYC, access logs, health, disciplinary), employee rights under DPDP S.11–14, consent register for discretionary processing, and third-party HR sharing. Distinct from Training Template T-19.
Board-Approved HR Data Policy + Acknowledgement Log

3.2 — Notice & Consent (Weeks 10-16)

Privacy Notice (Web)
T-07
Standalone, itemised privacy notice listing each processing purpose, legal basis (S.6 or S.7), data categories, retention period, and user rights. Available in 22 Indian languages.
Published Notice + Version History
Consent Form (FSIUU)
T-08
Per-purpose, granular consent form compliant with Section 6 FSIUU requirements. Supports withdrawal mechanism, audit trail, and consent artifact download by Data Principals.
Live Consent System + Audit Log
Retrospective Notice & Reconsent
T-09
Campaign to re-collect consent from existing users whose data was gathered before DPDP implementation. Covers email, SMS, and in-app notice workflows with 30-day re-consent window.
Reconsent Campaign + Outcomes Report
Section 7 Legitimate Uses Memo
T-06
Legal memo documenting processing activities that operate under Section 7 exemptions (KYC/AML, loan default assessment, employee BGV, RBI regulatory reporting, fraud detection). FinTech-critical.
Legal Sign-Off Memo

3.3 — Data Principal Rights (Weeks 12-20)

DSAR Request Form & Portal
T-10
Data Subject Access Request system — Data Principals can submit requests for data access, correction, or erasure. Enforces 90-day SLA with automated tracking and escalation alerts.
Live DSAR Portal (90-day SLA Active)
DSAR Response Letter
T-11
Standardised response letter for data access requests including full data export (JSON/PDF), processing summary, consent history, and instructions for exercising further rights.
Response Template (Multilingual)
Erasure & Correction SOP
T-12
Step-by-step procedures for executing data deletion and correction requests — including downstream system propagation, backup handling, and immutable deletion log generation.
Automated Deletion Workflow
Grievance Handling SOP
T-13
Complaint redressal system under Section 13. Covers intake, acknowledgement, investigation, resolution, and escalation to the Data Protection Board if unresolved within 90 days.
Live Grievance Portal (90-day SLA)
Nominee Registration Form
T-14
Allows Data Principals to designate a nominee who can exercise their data rights in the event of death or incapacity. Covers registration, identity verification, and delegation management.
Nominee Registry System
Section 9 Children's Data Policy
T-06A
Age-verification and parental consent framework for organisations serving minors. Prohibits behavioural tracking, targeted advertising, and profiling. Required for student loans, junior accounts, family apps.
Age-Gate + Parental Consent Flow

3.4 — Vendors & Processors (Weeks 14-20)

Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
T-15
Mandatory contract template for all third-party data processors under Section 8(2). Covers processing scope, security obligations, sub-processor controls, audit rights, and breach notification duties.
Signed DPAs with All Processors
Vendor Risk Assessment
T-16
Due diligence checklist assessing each third-party processor's security posture, data location, breach history, sub-processor usage, and DPDP compliance status before signing DPA.
Vendor Risk Register
Sub-Processor Register
T-17
Live register of all fourth-party (sub-processor) relationships — vendors used by your processors. Enforces notification requirements when sub-processors are added or changed.
Sub-Processor Transparency Register
Cross-Border Transfer Register
T-44
Tracks all transfers of personal data outside India. Enforces government country whitelist (Section 16). Triggers legal review if a destination country is removed from the approved list.
Transfer Register + Country Approval Log
Third-Party Inventory
T-25A
Master register of all vendors and processors who receive or process personal data. Captures data categories, DPA status, risk rating (High/Medium/Low), assessment frequency, and onboarding/offboarding checklist. Companion to T-15 DPA, T-16 Vendor Risk Assessment, T-17 Sub-Processor Register, and T-44 Cross-Border Register.
Live Vendor Inventory + DPA Status Tracker

3.5 — Breach Management (Weeks 16-22)

Incident Response Plan
T-20
End-to-end breach response playbook covering detection, containment, assessment, notification (72-hour DPB deadline), and recovery. Includes role assignments, escalation tree, and communication templates.
Signed IRP + On-Call Runbook
Breach Notification — DPB
T-21
Template notification to the Data Protection Board within 72 hours of breach discovery. Covers incident summary, data categories affected, estimated principal count, and immediate mitigation steps.
DPB Notification Template (72-hr)
Breach Notification — Data Principals
T-22
User-facing breach notification template explaining what data was compromised, the risk to the individual, steps taken to protect them, and actions they should take to protect themselves.
User Notification (Email + SMS)
Breach Register
T-30
Immutable, hash-chained log of all breach events with timeline, scope, response actions, notifications sent, and lessons learned. Required for Board and regulatory audit review.
Hash-Chained Breach Log

3.6 — Data Retention & Deletion (Weeks 18-22)

Deletion Log
T-29
Automated, immutable record of every data deletion event — triggered by DSAR erasure requests, consent withdrawal, or retention expiry. Includes 48-hour advance notice to affected principals.
Automated Deletion Audit Trail
Statutory Hold Register
T-43
Tracks mandatory legal retention obligations (RBI 8yr, PMLA 5yr, GST 6yr, CERT-In 180-day) that override DPDP deletion requirements. Prevents premature deletion of legally-held data.
Active Hold Register
Data Retention Schedule
T-02A
Per-category retention schedule mapping every data category to its retention period, legal basis (consent / statutory / legitimate use), deletion trigger, disposal method, and responsible owner. Covers customer data (10 categories), employee/HR data, and system logs. Includes statutory override logic and annual review log. Referenced by both Governance (Template #2) and Operations (Template #31).
DPO-Approved Retention Schedule + Annual Review Log
Processor Deletion Confirmation
T-33A
End-to-end deletion instruction and receipt workflow for processors. Includes: formal written instruction letter to processor (with Schedule A scope table), processor's signed confirmation receipt (with method, backup purge, sub-processor cascade), tracking log for all deletions, and 4-tier overdue escalation protocol (Day 1→30). Mandatory after every DSAR erasure, consent withdrawal, or contract termination.
Signed Deletion Receipts + Processor Confirmation Log

3.7 — Security Hardening (Weeks 9-24, Ongoing)

Security Hardening Plan
T-24
Comprehensive plan implementing AES-256-GCM encryption at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit, field-level encryption for sensitive PII (Aadhaar, PAN, biometrics), and key rotation schedule.
Encryption Deployment Report
Access Control Policy
T-34
Role-Based and Attribute-Based Access Control (RBAC/ABAC) framework covering admin roles (DPO, Legal, Compliance, Viewer), separation of duties, MFA enforcement, and privilege review cadence.
RBAC Policy + Access Matrix
Audit Logging Configuration
T-35
Hash-chained, tamper-evident audit log covering all consent events, DSAR actions, data access, deletions, and admin operations. Stored in separate, write-protected audit storage.
Immutable Audit Log System
Encryption Implementation Checklist
T-33
Verification checklist confirming AES-256 at-rest encryption, TLS 1.2+ in transit, field-level encryption for sensitive fields, and proper key management practices are all implemented.
Encryption Attestation Report

Phase 3 Complete

All gaps remediated. Consent system live. DSAR portal operational. DPAs signed. Breach IRP tested. Security hardening deployed. Phase 4 audit may commence.

Phase 4: Validation & Audit

11 Weeks (Wks 25-35)
17 Domains Re-Audited
Compliance Audit Plan
T-25
Structured internal audit plan covering all 17 DPDP compliance domains. Defines audit scope, sampling methodology, evidence requirements, and timeline for each sub-domain review.
Signed Audit Scope Document
Audit Findings Report
T-28
Formal audit findings with domain-by-domain scores, residual risks, open action items, and evidence references. Used by external auditors (SDF) and forms the basis for the compliance opinion.
Audit Report + Remediation Tracker
DPIA Workbook
T-26
Data Protection Impact Assessment workbook for high-risk processing operations (SDF mandatory). Covers risk identification, likelihood/impact scoring, mitigation controls, and DPO sign-off workflow.
Completed DPIA (SDF Mandatory)
Tabletop Breach Drill
T-20
Simulated breach incident exercise testing the end-to-end Incident Response Plan. Evaluates detection speed, 72-hour DPB notification SLA, communication quality, and post-incident review process.
Drill Results + IRP Amendments
Annual SDF Audit Report
T-36
Independent third-party audit report required annually for Significant Data Fiduciaries under Section 10. Covers all 17 domains with auditor attestation and board submission requirement.
Independent Auditor Report (SDF Only)
Consent Registry Dashboard
T-31
Real-time dashboard showing all consent events, withdrawal rates, FSIUU compliance scores per purpose, and any anomalies indicating consent hygiene issues requiring remediation.
Live Consent Monitoring Dashboard
Board Risk Committee Report
T-37
Executive-level compliance status presentation for the Board Risk Committee. Covers domain scores, open risks, penalty exposure summary, remediation progress, and sign-off recommendations.
Board Presentation Pack
Compliance Tracking Dashboard
T-38
Operational dashboard tracking remediation progress across all domains, outstanding action items, SLA adherence (DSAR, Grievance), and real-time compliance score.
Live Compliance Dashboard
DPIA Register
T-46
Log of all DPIA assessments conducted with dates, processing activities assessed, DPO sign-off, risk outcomes, and residual risk acceptance. Required for annual audit under Section 10.
DPIA Audit Trail (SDF Only)

Phase 4 Complete

Internal audit complete. No critical findings open. Tabletop drill passed. Board has reviewed and acknowledged the compliance status report. Ready for certification.

Phase 5: Certification

5 Weeks (Wks 36-40)
Compliance Opinion Letter
T-41
Formal compliance opinion issued by the engagement lead confirming successful remediation across all 17 DPDP domains. Constitutes the client's primary evidence of compliance for regulators and auditors.
Signed, Dated Opinion Letter
Board Resolution — Compliance
T-40
Formal board resolution acknowledging receipt of the compliance opinion, approving the ongoing obligations calendar, and committing to annual review cycles. Provides legal protection and accountability.
Signed Board Resolution
Compliance Status Report (Board Pack)
T-47
Executive summary for the Board and senior leadership detailing final remediation status, domain scores, residual risks with acceptance rationale, and forward compliance obligations.
Board Pack + Indexed Evidence File
Ongoing Obligations Calendar
T-45
Documented annual schedule of recurring DPDP obligations: consent audits (quarterly), DSAR SLA reviews (monthly), DPIA refresh (annual), staff training renewal, DPA review cycle, and SDF audit dates.
Operational Compliance Calendar
Consent Audit Worksheet
T-42
Final FSIUU verification audit across a sample of consent records to confirm the live consent system meets all Section 6 requirements. Serves as ongoing compliance spot-check tool.
Consent Quality Attestation
Privacy Committee Charter (Final)
T-39
Final, board-ratified version of the Privacy Committee Charter establishing the permanent governance body for ongoing DPDP compliance. Transitions the engagement from project to operations mode.
Ratified Charter + Inaugural Minutes
Statutory Hold Register (Final)
T-43
Final version of the statutory hold register, verified against current RBI/PMLA/GST/CERT-In obligations. Handed to the internal compliance team for ongoing maintenance.
Verified Hold Register
DPIA Register (Final)
T-46
Complete DPIA register documenting all impact assessments conducted during the engagement, DPO sign-offs, and residual risk acceptances. Handed to DPO for annual maintenance. (SDF mandatory.)
DPIA Register Handoff (SDF Only)
Compliance Evidence Pack
T-47
Indexed archive of all compliance evidence organised by domain: signed policies, consent logs, audit reports, DPAs, DSAR records, breach register, training certificates, and board resolutions.
Indexed Evidence Archive

Engagement Certified

Maximum DPDP Compliance Achieved. Evidence packed and risk registers accepted.
