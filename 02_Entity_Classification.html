Entity Classification Matrix
Determine Data Fiduciary (DF) or Significant Data Fiduciary (SDF) status
Purpose: Classify your organization to determine which DPDP obligations apply. This affects governance structure, audit requirements, and compliance timeline.

Classification Overview

Category Data Fiduciary (DF) Significant Data Fiduciary (SDF)
Definition Any entity processing DPDP-covered personal data Designated by Central Government based on criteria (10M+ users, sensitive data, national security)
Mandatory Obligations Sections 5–14 Sections 5–14 + Section 10 (DPO, DPIA, Audit)
Governance Required Privacy Committee (optional but recommended) DPO (mandatory), Privacy Committee
Audit Requirement Internal compliance audit (Consultant-led) Annual independent audit + internal audit
DPIA Requirement For high-risk processing only For all high-risk processing + formal DPIA register
Board Reporting Recommended Mandatory (annual audit + DPIA reports)

SDF Designation Criteria

Your organization is likely to be designated SDF if ANY of the following apply:

Criterion Trigger Examples
User Scale Processing personal data of 10M+ Data Principals in India Large social media, e-commerce, fintech, telecom, banking platforms
Sensitive Data Volume Processing sensitive data (health, financial, biometric, religious) at national scale Healthcare platforms, insurance companies, payment systems, government services
National Security Impact Processing impacts national security, critical infrastructure, or essential services Telecom operators, power grids, defense contractors, critical infrastructure
Cross-Border Transfers Regular transfers of Indian personal data to foreign countries Multinational companies, outsourced services to third countries
Important: As of April 7, 2026, the Government has NOT formally designated any organization as SDF. Designation expected after DPDP Rules 2025 implementation. Monitor Government notifications regularly.

Classification Self-Assessment

Answer the questions below to determine your likely classification:

Question DF Likely SDF
How many Indian Data Principals' data do you process? < 10M > 10M
Do you process sensitive data (health, financial, biometric)? Limited scale National scale
Are you regulated by RBI, SEBI, IRDAI, or other critical sector regulator? One or few Multiple / Critical sectors
Do you transfer Indian personal data to foreign countries regularly? Rarely / None Regularly / Significant volume
Would a data breach impact critical infrastructure or essential services? No Yes
Are you a major platform (social media, e-commerce, fintech, telecom)? No Yes
