SECTION 16 RESTRICTION: DPDP Act Section 16 requires that personal data of Indian residents CANNOT be transferred outside India unless the destination country is whitelisted by the Central Government. As of now, NO countries are whitelisted. The government notification is pending. All cross-border transfers are currently NOT PERMITTED unless they fall under narrow exceptions.

CONTRACTUAL SAFEGUARD TRANSFERS (Pending Whitelisting)

If destination country is not whitelisted, Section 16 DOES NOT permit transfer UNLESS it falls under narrow exceptions (vital interests, public interest). Contractual safeguards (DPA) alone do NOT satisfy Section 16.
