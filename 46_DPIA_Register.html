DPIA CONTENT CHECKLIST
Required DPIA Section Purpose / Content Mandatory?
1. Processing Activity Description What data? For what purpose? By whom? For how long? Yes
2. Necessity & Proportionality Assessment Is this processing necessary for the stated purpose? Is data collected proportionate to purpose? Yes
3. Data Principal Rights Impact How does this processing affect data principals' rights (access, correct, erase, withdraw consent)? Yes
4. Risk Identification & Rating Identify all risks to data principals (breach, misuse, unauthorized access, profiling) Yes
5. Privacy by Design Measures Encryption, access controls, data minimization, pseudonymization, technical safeguards Yes
6. Mitigation Strategy How will identified risks be mitigated? What controls are in place? Yes
7. Residual Risk Acceptance After mitigation, what residual risks remain? Who accepts them? Yes
8. DPO Sign-Off DPO reviews and formally approves the DPIA Yes
9. Board / Senior Management Approval Processing only commences after Board approval of DPIA findings Yes
DPIA RISK RATING SCALE
Rating Likelihood × Impact Typical Processing Activities Board Escalation Required?
CRITICAL High likelihood × High impact (e.g., 4×4 = 16) Biometric processing, health data, children's data, profiling YES — Board must approve before processing
HIGH High/Medium likelihood × High impact (e.g., 3×4, 4×3) KYC verification, cross-border transfers, third-party sharing YES — Board sign-off required
MEDIUM Medium likelihood × Medium impact (e.g., 2×2, 3×2, 2×3) Regular customer data, employee records, transaction logs Optional — DPO approval sufficient; inform Board
LOW Low likelihood × Low impact (e.g., 1×2, 2×1) Anonymized data, aggregated statistics, non-sensitive metadata No — DPO approval sufficient
