1. Purpose & Legal Basis (DPDP Section 17)

Legal Requirement: DPDP Act 2023 Section 17 mandates that personal data be retained only "as long as necessary" for stated purposes. This Deletion Log creates an immutable audit trail proving compliance with deletion obligations.

Purpose: Track every erasure request, deletion execution, and processor confirmation. Hash-chained rows detect tampering. 7-year WORM archive proves historical compliance.

2. Deletion Log Schema (12-Field Record)

Field Type Purpose
deletion_id UUID Unique identifier (primary key)
principal_id HASH (SHA-256) Data principal identifier (hashed for privacy)
deletion_type ENUM Erasure (Section 12) | Expiry (Section 17) | Legal Hold Exception
data_categories TEXT (JSON) Categories deleted: ["Name", "Email", "Aadhaar_VID"]
deletion_reason TEXT Erasure request | Retention period expired | RtBF | Withdrawal
request_date TIMESTAMP When deletion was requested
executed_date TIMESTAMP When deletion was completed (SLA: ≤30 days)
deletion_method TEXT Crypto-shredding | Physical destruction | Anonymization
processors_notified TEXT (JSON) ["Vendor_A", "Vendor_B"] — processors instructed to delete
processor_confirmations TEXT (JSON) Confirmation date per processor (SLA: 5 business days per Section 12(4))
prev_hash SHA-256 Hash-chaining: SHA-256 of previous row; detect tampering
authorized_by TEXT DPO/Compliance officer who approved deletion

3. Deletion Workflow (7 Steps, ≤30 Day SLA)

T+0 (Receipt): Deletion request received (erasure demand, retention expiry, withdrawal)
T+1 (Verification): Verify principal identity; confirm data categories to be deleted
T+2 (Assessment): Check Statutory Hold Register — if hold exists, notify principal of exception
T+5 (Execution): Execute deletion in all primary systems; record method in log
T+6 (Processors): Send deletion instruction to all processors with 5-day confirmation deadline
T+11 (Confirmation): Collect deletion confirmations from processors (Section 12(4) propagation SLA)
T+30 (Close): Close deletion request; archive log entry; update Compliance Dashboard

4. Immutability & Hash-Chaining

Database Constraint (INSERT-ONLY): deletion_log table has NO UPDATE/DELETE permissions (even for DBA). All changes are new rows.

Hash-Chaining: Each row includes prev_hash = SHA-256(previous_row). Tampers are detected: modifying a row changes its hash, breaking the chain downstream.

WORM Archive: After 90 days, deletion_log entries archived to write-once storage (S3 Object Lock, WORM tape) — no deletion possible even with root access.

5. Exception Handling (Statutory Hold)

Category Retention Period Legal Basis Action on Erasure Request
KYC Records (NBFC/Bank) 10 years RBI Master Direction Hold; explain in response letter; offer anonymization
Loan/Credit Records 8 years post-closure RBI Master Direction Hold; explain in response letter
Tax Records 6 years Income Tax Act 1961 Hold; explain in response letter
Litigation Hold Until case closed + appeals exhausted Court order / Legal privilege Hold; explain in response letter; escalate to Legal
