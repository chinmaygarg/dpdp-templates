Purpose & Legal Basis: Under Section 8(2) of the DPDP Act 2023, a Data Fiduciary must ensure that any Data Processor it engages processes personal data only as instructed and implements appropriate technical and organisational security measures. This register provides a consolidated view of all third parties (vendors, service providers, partners) who receive or process personal data on behalf of the organisation. It is the master companion to the Sub-Processor Register (Template #23), the Vendor Risk Assessment (Template #22), and the Data Processing Agreement (Template #21).
MANDATORY: No vendor may begin processing personal data until: (1) this register entry is complete, (2) a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is signed, and (3) a Vendor Risk Assessment is completed and approved. Engaging a vendor without these steps constitutes a DPDP Act violation.
