Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?
40 weeks (9-10 months) from start to certificate. Can be compressed with parallel work, extended if organization is large/complex.
What if we're SDF?
+6 SDF-specific templates, +external annual auditor, +DPO appointment, +formal DPIA. Adds ~4-6 weeks to timeline.
What does the certificate mean?
Consultant's opinion that organization is ≥85% DPDP-compliant. Board-approved. Shows good-faith compliance effort for regulators.
What happens after certification?
Ongoing Obligations: annual DPIA review, staff training refresh, consent audit, board reporting (Template 45).
Can we start before Phase 0 is done?
No. DPDP applicability must be confirmed first (legal + CFO). If DPDP doesn't apply, effort is redirected to state privacy laws.
How much will it cost?
Depends on organization size and current maturity. Typically: Small (₹10-20L) | Mid (₹25-50L) | Enterprise (₹50L+)
